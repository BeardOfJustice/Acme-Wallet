Our Vision
The most profound ideas are often the simplest, and ours has developed from several connected and fundamental beliefs:

  • The cryptocurrency user experience needs to be simplified.
  • We believe this can be done from a single interface.
  • Users should be rewarded for keeping the assets in their own custody.
 This is our vision, and we want to take some time to share it with you.

The Problem
Cryptocurrency users expose themselves to multiple risks and obstacles when owning, holding and trading cryptocurrencies:

  • Moving money around wallets and to different exchanges and wed-interfaces involves multiple security risks.
  • It makes it difficult to keep track of one’s assets and provides a poor user experience.
  • Multiple wallets are needed to hold coins from different blockchains. Each wallet adds complexity to the user experience and reduces security.

Introducing ACME Wallet

We decided to fix DeFi.

Imagine being able to stake tokens in your own wallet, receiving rewards and managing your assets from any chain, all in one place. The coins never leave your custody.

The mantra “Not your keys, not your coins” has been lost somewhat in the rush to send coins to smart contracts in return for rewards. ACME wallet aims to eliminate this by providing an in-wallet rewards for retention of coins. Projects benefit from the incentive-based lock-up of those tokens, and users benefit through asset appreciation, security and ease of use.
Token & Integration
The $ACME token is the native token of the ecosystem. It acts to reduce the fees on on-chain transactions. Your transactions require $ACME, just like they require gas, and $ACME is distributed as the reward for anyone holding supported tokens in their wallet.

As the user base grows, so will the rewards. The platform is token-agnostic: any project will be able to integrate and ensure that holding their tokens is incentivised through $ACME rewards.

How Integration works

Any project will be able to integrate with the ACME wallet, so that their users can deposit tokens and experience custodial staking. Projects have a massive incentive to do this, as holders will be rewarded based on a time-weighted holding calculation, so the longer you hold, the better the returns. It keeps a user’s tokens entirely liquid, while reducing sell pressure.

To integrate, projects will purchase $ACME and deposit it into a contract that will designate the tokens for rewards distribution. The virtuous circle this creates is a buy-pressure on $ACME and further incentive to use the wallet and hold that project's token, while expanding the ACME Wallet user base to another following.
Partnerships

Just create a wallet and put your tokens in it to experience the first ever self-custodial staking experience in DeFi. It really is a crypto purist’s dream.
Asset Management
For those who want to move their token holdings, there is no “unstaking” involved. Although holders are rewarded, your assets remain entirely liquid, with coin-to-coin swaps being implemented at low fees.
